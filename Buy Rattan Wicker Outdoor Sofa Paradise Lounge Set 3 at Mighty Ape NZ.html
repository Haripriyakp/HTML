Rattan Wicker Outdoor Sofa Paradise Lounge Setting (4-Piece Set) –
Beige/Brown



This contemporary rattan-style outdoor furniture set brings an elegant touch
to your backyard, patio, deck, pool or entertaining area. Constructed with a
lightweight aluminium frame with brown wicker and beige cushions, you’ll find
it hard to do anything but relax in this stunning contemporary setting. With a
classic rattan weave, these suites are weather-resistant and UV tested for New
Zealand conditions.



Features:



    
	
  • Stylish, open weave rattan outdoor lounge set
    • 

	
  • Durable, low-maintenance UV stabilized resin construction
    • 

	
  • Weatherproof, washable cushions
    • 

	
  • Versatile coffee table with 5mm clear tempered glass
    • 

	
  • Lightweight aluminium frame
    • 

	
  • Basic assembly required
    • 




Specifications:



    
	
  • 1 x Double Sofa: 121×69×78cm
		
      
			
    • 2 x Seat Cushion: 94×53×5cm
      • 
		
    
	
    • 

	
  • 2 x Single Sofa: 63×69×78cm
		
      
			
    • 2 x Seat Cushion: 94×53×5cm
      • 
		
    
	
    • 

	
  • 1 x Coffee Table 92×46×35cm with 5mm Clear Tempered Glass
    • 

	
  • Wicker Colour: Brown
    • 

	
  • Cushion Colour: Beige
    • 

	
  • Fabric: 180g Polyester
    • 

	
  • Carton size & weight (Approx.) :
		
      
			
    • 124×80×41cm, 30.0Kg
      • 
		
    
	
    • 




Note:

Product colour may slightly vary due to photographic lighting sources or your
monitor settings. Decorative items not included.

How it works

            
    
                
  • 
                    
                        Simply select Afterpay as your payment method in the checkout or when picking up your items from the Mighty Ape distribution centre,
                        and enter your details at Afterpay to complete your order
                    
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    Your payment schedule will be shown in the checkout, and emailed to you
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    If you are a new Afterpay customer, the first payment will be made at the time of purchase, with
                    remaining payments over the next 6 weeks
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    Once you have been an Afterpay customer for 6 weeks, on subsequent orders you may be eligible to
                    make your first payment in 14 days, with final payment in 8 weeks
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    If you need to return your items for a refund, the payment plan can be cancelled
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    If you fail to make a payment, you will be charged a late payment fee of $10 with a further $7
                    fee added 7 days later if the payment is still unpaid
                
    •
