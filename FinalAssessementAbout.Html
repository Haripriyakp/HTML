About Us

Lauren Kanungo

Lauren Kanungo , She is the CEO of My Interiors Company.She established My Interiors company in April 1999.
Lauren began her interior design career at a healthcare architecture firm where she learned how to balance
the artistic side of the profession with the technical demands required to document and implement the intended
vision. Since then, she's enjoyed working as both a general commercial designer and as a hospitality focused
designer.Her passion lies in creating concept-based, comfortable interior environments that feel naturally balanced.
Her goal is to create a space that represents the client, brand, or user while being appreciative of the costs,
materials, and time required to make it a reality.
